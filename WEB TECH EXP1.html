Oranges

Always fill your heart with joy

The fruit is a modified berry known as a hesperidium, and the flesh is divided into segments called carpels.
The usual shape of the sweet-orange fruit is round and the colour of its pulp orange,
but there are variations. The mandarin, for example, is distinctly flattened, and the blood orange has red pulp.
