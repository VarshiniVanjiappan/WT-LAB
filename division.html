OUR STORY

There are not many experiences that are as satisfying as digging into a good burger.
For some people it practically becomes a spiritual experience as with each bite the symphony
of flavours dancing on their taste buds ignites deeply fulfilling pleasure centers
like a nice tight hug.

Welcome to Burgerama

With this in mind, three childhood friends in Gurgaon set out to recreate their
idea of the perfect burger experience in the year 2018.

twenty year long friendship and some of their most cherished memories are diving into secret
